Info Page

HairToShare is an IOS, Android and Web application. Its goals is to connect hair donors to societies like Wigs for Kids that will take this hair and provide the hair to patients who have gone through chemotherapy and have lost hair as a result. This will empower those kids and people who have gone through this. If you want to be part of this amazing experience, click the button below:
